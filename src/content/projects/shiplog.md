## What it is

Shiplog is a publishing format for people who build in public but do not want their updates to feel like a noisy social feed.

## Problem

Project timelines are often fragmented across commits, chats, screenshots, and temporary notes. That makes it hard to see what actually shipped and why.

## Approach

Each update is structured around decisions, progress, risks, and next steps, so the output stays readable both as a running log and as future project documentation.

## Outcome

The concept is promising because it creates a cleaner bridge between working notes and public communication.
